Lemon rice is excellent for lunchboxes because the rice stays separate and the tempering remains fragrant even after cooling.

## Instructions

1. Spread cooked rice on a plate so it cools without clumping.
2. Heat oil and crackle mustard seeds.
3. Add peanuts, curry leaves, and turmeric; fry until the peanuts turn crisp.
4. Fold in rice and salt.
5. Switch off the heat and mix in lemon juice.

## Variations

Add grated ginger for warmth, green chilli for heat, or roasted cashews for a richer festival version.
